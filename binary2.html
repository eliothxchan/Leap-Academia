in electronics. Through circuits we run elextricity, so that all
our gadgets and what not can turn on. Circuits can only be on or
off, there's no inbetween. Computers have thousands of circuits
in them, and they count by turning these on and off.

When a circuit is turned on, the computer thinks of it as a 1,
when it is off the compuer thinks of it as a 0. Now, you're
